Welcome to the world of gourmet food, simplified.

Let's start with a fact – our time here is limited. And it's a shame that we must either value our time and compromise on our food, or spend an awful lot of time putting together meals.

This is especially true for gourmet food. Rich broths take hours to develop. Great soups can't be rushed. Proper pasta sauces are layered, balanced and slow-cooked to perfection. Gourmet food has many wonderful qualities. Impatience isn't one of them.

So the food industry found a different solution. Real ingredients became powders. Slow-cooked flavour became flavour mimicking. Wholesome ingredients gave way to fillers and preservatives.

Convenient? Absolutely.
Gourmet? Not even close.

That's why we created Brothsmen.

From soups, pasta sauces and ramen broths, every Brothsmen product is crafted with real ingredients and made ready-to-eat, so you can enjoy gourmet food without spending hours preparing it. No added preservatives. No flavour shortcuts. No compromise.

Our Range

Creamy Mushroom Soup

Farm fresh mushrooms, caramelised and slow cooked with onion, garlic and thyme, blended with a dollop of cream to give you a deliciously velvety soup. Enjoy the flavours of earthy, wholesome ingredients and real mushrooms in every bite. Pair it with garlic bread and enjoy a yummy meal! 100% vegetarian.

Tomato & Basil Soup

This classic favourite is made from plump tomatoes, fresh basil, sauteed garlic and cream, perfectly blended together. Tangy and flavourful, this hearty comfort soup comes with an Italian twist. Add toasted croutons and you’ve got yourself a delicious meal! 100% vegetarian.

Classic Chicken Soup

Made with chunks of juicy tender chicken, seared in garlic and butter, topped with cream. Italian and French flavours combine in this protein-rich velvety soup. Pair it with garlic bread and you’ve got yourself a delicious meal.
